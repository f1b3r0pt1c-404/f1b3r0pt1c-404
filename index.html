Executive Summary

This proposal offers the opportunity to purchase a profitable and well-established laundry room business located in the Mercury Hotel - All Suites, in the heart of South Beach, Miami Beach, Florida. The business serves hotel guests and local residents, is primed for continued profitability and room for growth, providing convenient laundry services with minimal operational costs and stable weekly profits. The nearby beach and hotel bar offer additional amenities to enhance customer experience

Business Overview

  • Business Name: Mercury Hotel Laundry Room
  • Location: Mercury Hotel - All Suites, South Beach, Miami Beach, Florida
  • Services Offered: Self-service laundry for both hotel guests and local residents.
  • History: Successfully operating for several years, this business is integrated with the hotel. Serving conveniently customers from it and surrounding areas, this laundry room has built a reliable and growing customer base as a solution to needs.
  • Ownership Structure: Currently owner-operated.

Financial Overview

  • Revenue: Weekly profits range from a minimum of $500, with potential for more depending on seasonal demand and marketing efforts.
  • Operating Costs: Rent is $100 per month, with other costs primarily related to utilities and equipment maintenance.
  • Assets:
    • 3 washing machines
    • 3 dryer machines
    • 1 vending machine for laundry products (Payment Systems: Accepts both card and payments.)
  • Liabilities: No significant outstanding debts.

Customer and Market Overview

  • Target Customers: Primarily hotel guests and local residents.
  • Contract: The business operates under a 10-year contract with the Mercury Hotel, ensuring consistent foot traffic and revenue.
  • Additional Amenities: Customers can enjoy the hotel’s first-floor bar while waiting, or take advantage of the nearby beach, located just a few blocks away, adding to the customer experience.
  • Market Potential: This location benefits from high foot traffic due to tourism and local demand, offering significant growth opportunities.

Operations Overview

  • Day-to-day Operations: Self-service laundry facility, accessible 7 days a week, 24 hours.
  • Staffing: No staff required for daily operations, minimizing overhead costs.
  • Equipment Condition: The washers, dryers, and vending machine are in good condition, with no immediate need for replacement or repair. (Information for service if needed will be provided)
  • Liabilities: No significant outstanding debts.

Growth Potential

  • Expansion Opportunities: Increase marketing efforts targeting local residents, or establish partnerships with nearby businesses or hotels to expand the customer base.
  • Service Expansion: Explore offering additional services such as pick-up and delivery, dry cleaning partnerships, or additional vending options in order to increase sales.

Reason for Selling

  • The current owner is seeking to sell due to personal reasons, providing an incoming owner with a profitable and established business that requires minimal hands-on management.
